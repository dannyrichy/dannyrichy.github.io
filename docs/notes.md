### Gaussian Process

A gaussian process is a stochastic process such that any \$$ {X_t \| t \in \mathcal{T}} $$, forms a multivariate Gaussian Distribution. \$$ \mathcal{T} $$ is any collection of time steps. [Read here](notes/gaussian_process.md)
